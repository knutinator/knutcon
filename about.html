About us

Knut's Construction has its roots in the 1960's, when Knut Halvarsson, the grandfather of the current owner, opened up a small wood workshop in the outskirts of Göteborg. The company grew and grew over the years, and in 1997 we opened up the current office and workshop building in the heart of Majorna, where we have been situated since.

The company currently employs over 100 builders, carpenters and craftsmen. We have collected a wide range of knowledge and expertice under the same roof in order to be able to offer the highest quality of construction work to our customers. We are constantly looking for more talent to add to our ever-expanding workforce.

Where to find us

Do you have a building project or idea you need help with? We invite you to come down to our workshop for a cup of coffee and a chat about what we can offer you.

No matter how big or small your need is, we always find a solution that combines the highest craftmanship with reasonable pricing. Because we believe that fine carpentry is for everyone.
